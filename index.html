Code for Social Good

With the rapid advancement of technology, it is important to use its power in ways that benefit society. Hackathon challenges its participants to code for social good, and create an opportunity for us to explore the intersection between technology and society.

On February 22-24, over 700 students, hackers, and creators will come together for 48 hours of coding. For the 3rd year in a row, we are bringing the most talented students in India to address the state's most pressing issues. Participants can choose to build a project that solves a problem in one of our live themes.

Health

Develop a product that encourages people to live a healthier lifestyle. Help predict and prevent illnesses before they affect the body.

Environment

Aggregate public data to analyze or improve government green energy policies. Design solutions to reduce energy consumption in habitable spaces.

Education

Discover new ways to faciliatate learning within the classroom. Enable accessible education for individuals with learning disabilities.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you transform your crazy ideas into real stuff. Thousands of coders across Dehradun and other parts of the INdia form teams around an idea and collaboratively create a solution from scratch. These ideas turn into websites, mobile apps, hardware, and more!

Come make the most incredible things you can imagine alongside fellow creators. You take care of building and we'll take care of you.

Who can attend?

Hackathon is open to all majors! As long as you're prepared to learn, brainstorm, and build cool things, we welcome you to join. We encourage participants to form teams of up to 4 people and a minimum of 2.

We will be holding workshops throughout the year for students to immerse themselves into the world of hackathons and technology. These workshops will be held by industry professionals and members of our team, who will teach new and upcoming tools that assist with web development, mobile development, and more.

What can I build?

You can build whatever your heart desires, whether its a web app, mobile app, or an open source tool. Any project under one of oyr live themes is recommended.

Our main focus is to bring you a learning experience where you can have fun, ask questions, and experiment with technology.
